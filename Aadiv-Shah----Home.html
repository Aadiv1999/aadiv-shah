About Me

Hello! I am an undergraduate student pursuing Electronics and Telecommunications Engineering from Veermata Jijabai Technological Institute, Mumbai. My penchant for robots stemmed from competitive robotics and has led me to pursue a career in Robotics and Systems Development.

My genesis as a thinker, innovator and designer originated when Hands On, Minds On, experiential learning became a way of life! My abilities to work with a team are uncontested and I believe that smart collaborative work with multidisciplinary experts (and machines!) accelerates societal development. 

I am working with Prof. Dr. Faruk Kazi on devising an autonomous quality-control mechanism using Computer Vision and Image Processing Algorithms in accordance with the Industry 4.0 paradigm. My final year project AgriAir , is targeted towards developing Path Planning Algorithms using Deep Reinforcement Learning to mitigate locust infestation in agricultural lands.
